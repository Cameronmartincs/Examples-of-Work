Getting Started

There are many sites with many different fantasy football applications are diffeerent ways of scoring.

I usually create my fantasy teams at www.nfl.com because they follow a standard that is used in the majority of sites.

To get started:

  1. Register an account with your e-mail at www.nfl.com
  2. Use the draft client to sign up for a draft time and league.
  3. Use the draft client to get players for your team.
  4. Set up starting lineup and follow your team to victory!

Keys To Success

These tips will show how utilizing resources built into the nfl website will help you win all year long.

Always Watch Your Matchups!

Making sure you play your best players against key matchup is the most important key to success.

Know Who Is Getting The Points

Keeping track of the amount of points each person recieves(both total and per game) will secure your starting team early in the season.

Know Thy Enemy

Understanding the general perception of what others are doing with their players will give you an edge on who to start, bench, and pick up.

Touches Matter!

Possibly the most important aspect of fantasy football is the amount of times a player is getting the ball. The more touches, the more opportunity for points.

Watch Your Games

The game center will update your projections and scored points in real-time. Make sure you watch as your team rises or falls. This is also a good way of determining if you need to switch a player out at the last minute to gain an edge.

Never Shy Away From Projections

The new preview section will give you an idea of how your games will go. Included are the projections of points and the players that will score them. Also gives an insight on your opponent.
